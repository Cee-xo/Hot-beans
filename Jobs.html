Junior Web Developer – Job Specification

About the Role

We are looking for enthusiastic and motivated Junior Web Developers to join our growing team. This role is ideal for individuals who have recently completed training courses, bootcamps, or formal qualifications in web development and are ready to begin their professional careers. You will work alongside experienced developers, contributing to real-world projects while continuing to develop your technical and professional skills.

Key Responsibilities

  • Assist in the development and maintenance of websites and web applications
  • Write clean, efficient, and well-documented code
  • Collaborate with designers and senior developers to implement features
  • Test and debug applications to ensure optimal performance
  • Participate in code reviews and team meetings
  • Stay up to date with emerging web technologies and best practices

Personal Attributes

  • Willingness to learn and take on feedback
  • Good communication and teamwork skills
  • Ability to manage time and meet deadlines
  • Positive attitude and enthusiasm for technology

What We Offer

  • A supportive environment for career development
  • Mentoring from experienced developers
  • Opportunities for training and progression
  • Competitive salary and benefits package
  • Flexible working options

Required Skills and Qualifications

  • Completion of a web development course, degree, or equivalent qualification
  • Basic knowledge of: HTML, CSS, and JavaScript; Responsive web design principles
  • Familiarity with at least one of the following: Front-end frameworks (e.g. React, Vue); Back-end development (e.g. Python, PHP)
  • Understanding of version control systems (e.g. Git)
  • Strong problem-solving skills and attention to detail
